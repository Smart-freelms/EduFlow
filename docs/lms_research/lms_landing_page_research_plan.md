# LMS Landing Page Analysis - Research Plan

## Objective
Analyze landing pages of successful LMS platforms to understand their page structure, messaging, visual design, CTA strategies, and positioning of free vs paid features.

- [x] Coursera (coursera.org)
- [x] Udemy (udemy.com)
